# Job Evaluation Skill

## Purpose
Evaluate incoming jobs against the candidate profile and return APPLY/DONT_APPLY.

## Profile Summary
Senior Machine Learning Engineer and Applied AI Lead with 9+ years of experience in AI systems across SaaS, fintech, and health-tech. Specializes in designing end-to-end AI product systems, focusing on model quality, retrieval correctness, and iterative evaluation. Proven track record in improving AI outputs and mentoring engineers.

## Core Strengths
- NLP and LLM expertise
- Strong experience in AI product development
- Cross-functional collaboration skills
- Proficient in Python and SQL
- Experience with distributed systems and cloud technologies

## Decision Heuristics
- Look for roles with a focus on AI and machine learning
- Prefer positions that involve collaborative work across teams
- Seek opportunities for mentoring and technical leadership
- Prioritize jobs that emphasize production quality and monitoring
- Find roles that involve retrieval and search-related technologies

## Decision Output Contract
- Decision: APPLY or DONT_APPLY
- Rationale: concise and evidence-based

## Feedback Updates
- Preference for hands-on roles in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and NLP with direct production ownership.
- Desire for measurable quality targets in job responsibilities.
- Interest in positions that allow for technical leadership and mentorship opportunities.
- Prioritize roles that involve collaborative work and cross-functional teams.
