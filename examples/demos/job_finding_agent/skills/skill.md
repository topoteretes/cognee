# Job Evaluation Skill

## Purpose
Evaluate incoming jobs against the candidate profile and return APPLY/DONT_APPLY.

## Profile Summary
Senior Machine Learning Engineer and Applied AI Lead with 9+ years of experience in building retrieval-heavy, production-grade AI systems across SaaS, fintech, and health-tech domains. Expert in NLP, search relevance, knowledge graphs, and LLM application reliability. Proven ability to turn research prototypes into measurable product impact in real production environments. Experience in designing and operating end-to-end AI product systems, focusing on model quality, retrieval correctness, latency, and safety at scale.

## Core Strengths
- NLP expertise
- Search relevance
- Knowledge graphs
- Model evaluation frameworks
- Production AI systems
- Cross-functional collaboration
- Technical mentoring
- AI product impact

## Decision Heuristics
- Look for roles that require hands-on technical development and production readiness.
- Preference for positions with a strong API, backend, or data-focused component.
- Consider companies in AI, SaaS, and health-tech industries that value iterative evaluation and retrieval systems.
- Seek opportunities that promote mentorship and leadership in engineering practices.
- Evaluate for positions emphasizing high-performance and reliability standards in AI applications.

## Decision Output Contract
- Decision: APPLY or DONT_APPLY
- Confidence: float in [0,1]
- Rationale: concise and evidence-based
